Core features

1) Comprehensive Network Status

Quick view of your active connection, type, SSID, and local IP address directly on a clean, modern dashboard.

2) Hotspot QR Code

Easily manage and view your TV's Hotspot credentials, generating a scannable QR Code for seamless connection.

3) Native Speed Test

Accurate, built-in network testing featuring True ICMP Ping, Download/Upload speeds, and automatic SD/HD/4K stream quality ratings.

4) Advanced Utilities

Fast shortcuts to Wi-Fi settings, OEM power management, developer permissions, and legacy tools.

Quick setup

  1. Download the app directly from your TV's Google Play Store.
  2. Open the app to instantly view your network status.
  3. Use the on-screen buttons to quickly jump to specific TV settings or run a Speed Test.

Known limitations

Android TV behavior differs by OEM and firmware version. Some shortcut intents are device-specific. The app provides best-effort fallbacks but cannot guarantee identical behavior on every model.
