Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Amit Tabul ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Stream Keep Alive (TV Connectivity Hub) mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our Service is designed as a local diagnostic and connectivity utility for Android TV. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. App Functionality and Local Data

The Service operates primarily on your local device. It may request specific Android system permissions (such as Accessibility, Notification Access, or Location) strictly to provide its core functionality, which involves managing local network connections, automating screen interactions, or reading local device state. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and services to facilitate our Service or to analyze how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Analytics and Speed Tests

The Service provides a speed test feature that connects to public Content Delivery Networks (such as Cloudflare) to measure download and upload speeds. Standard network identifiers (like your IP address) are inherently exposed to these third-party testing servers during the measurement process, subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support mechanisms provided within the app or on our official website.
