# Permissions & Settings - TV Connectivity Hub

## Accessibility Service
Used for local utility automation and diagnostics.

## Notification Access (Recommended)
Improves playback/session awareness and telemetry quality.

## Battery Optimization Exemption (Recommended)
Improves continuity for long sessions on aggressive OEM power policies.

## Write Settings (Optional but Recommended)
Allows temporary display timeout extension during active utility sessions.
Original timeout is restored when the session stops.

## Important
These permissions improve reliability but do not guarantee identical behavior across all TV models/apps.
